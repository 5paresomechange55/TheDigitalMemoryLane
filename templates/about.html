About Us

Preserving your moments, one pixel at a time.

Welcome to The Digital Memory Lane — an eternal canvas for personal and collective digital memories. Our site allows users to purchase pixels on a digital grid, upload an image to commemorate the space, and vote for a charity. Once all pixels are filled, a donation will be made to the winning charity.

For families: Celebrate anniversaries, baby photos, and family milestones on a canvas that will stand the test of time.

For influencers: Mark your digital legacy and promote your brand creatively — forever on the grid.

For nostalgic souls: Whether it’s your first AOL screen name or a tribute to MySpace days, relive your favorite digital moments here.

For history lovers: Leave your mark in a modern-day digital time capsule, curated for future generations.
